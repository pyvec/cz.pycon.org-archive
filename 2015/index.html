PyCon CZ2015

Brno, Czech Republic

November 14-15

The very first PyCon in the Czech Republic. Join us for a fun weekend full of talks, sprints and workshops about Python.

Talks

Saturday 14th
Faculty of Information Technology,
Božetěchova 1/2

One day, two parallel tracks, two keynotes and ten awesome talks. Community, web, hardware, science, devops and more.

Sprints & workshops

Sunday 15th
Impact Hub Brno,
Cyrilská 7

Six workshops in two blocks and sprinting all day long. Hack on open-source projects together with other Python community members.
